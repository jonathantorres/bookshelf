### Exercise 1.13
If $n > 1$ then:  

```math
Fib(n+1) = Fib(n) + Fib(n-1) = \frac{\phi^n - \psi^n + \phi^{n-1} - \psi^{n-1}}{\sqrt{5}}
= \frac{\phi^{n-1} (\phi + 1) - \psi^{n-1}(\psi + 1)}{\sqrt{5}}
= \frac{\phi^{n-1}(\phi^2) - \psi^{n-1} (\psi^2)}{\sqrt{5}}
= \frac{\phi^{n+1} - \psi^{n+1}}{\sqrt{5}}
```
